About Us

Reliable mining support built on experience, proven projects, and a safety-first culture.

Who We Are

Thama Mining Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a B2B mining services and engineering contractor based in Mokopane, Limpopo. We support mine operators, processing plant managers, and facilities teams with hands-on technical services.

Our expertise spans HDPE pipe systems (butt welding & jointing), conveyor maintenance, equipment compliance (TMM brake testing), supply of mining consumables including quarry aggregates, and minor civil works.

We operate in the heart of South Africa's Waterberg mining belt — serving clients such as Ivanplats (Platreef), Anglo American's Mogalakwena mine, and Redpath Mining.
