About Us

Reliable mining support built on experience, safety, and fast response.

Who We Are

Thama Mining Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a B2B mining services and engineering contractor based in Mokopane, Limpopo. We support mine operators, processing plant managers, and facilities teams with on-site technical services.

Our expertise spans pipe systems, conveyor maintenance, equipment compliance (TMM brake testing), supply of mining consumables, and minor civil works. We pride ourselves on emergency call-out capability and a safety-first culture.

We operate in the heart of South Africa’s Waterberg mining belt, serving clients such as Anglo American’s Mogalakwena mine and Ivanhoe’s Platreef project.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, compliant, and responsive mining support services that minimise downtime and maximise operational efficiency for our clients.

Our Work Approach

  • Safety First: All work complies with Mine Health and Safety Act regulations.
  • Fast Response: Emergency call-outs available 7 days a week.
  • Technical Excellence: Skilled technicians in HDPE welding, conveyor splicing, and equipment testing.
  • Client Focus: “It’s less about us and more about you.”

Safety‑First Culture

In mining, safety is non‑negotiable. We ensure all our services — from TMM brake testing to conveyor maintenance — meet or exceed regulatory standards. Our team is trained in hazard identification, risk assessment, and PPE compliance.
