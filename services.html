Our Services

Comprehensive engineering, maintenance, and compliance solutions designed to eliminate downtime and keep your mine operational.

Pipe & Fluid Systems

Specialised pipeline infrastructure is critical for slurry, water, and chemical transfer in processing plants. We provide permanent, leak-proof joints and comprehensive maintenance to ensure continuous flow.

  • HDPE Butt Welding
  • Pipe & Valve Installations
  • Long-term Maintenance Contracts

Conveyor Systems

Conveyors are the lifeline of your production. A stopped belt means a stopped mine. Our rapid-response splicing and maintenance teams are built to respond fast and restore operations.

  • Hot & Cold Belt Splicing
  • Idlers & Pulley Supply and Installation
  • Conveyor Belt & Plant Cleaning

Mining Equipment Services

Compliance is non-negotiable. We offer legally required testing and dedicated workshop support to ensure your trackless mobile machinery meets all South African Mine Health & Safety Act regulations.

  • MHSA-Compliant TMM Brake Testing
  • Heavy Machinery Workshop Services
  • Processing Plant Support

Supply Division

Reliable procurement for critical operations. We supply mining tools, specialized engineering consumables, and equipment directly to your site, ensuring your teams never stop working due to a lack of gear.

Construction & Civil

Beyond mechanical support, we handle the on-site minor construction works and civil engineering support required to keep surface infrastructure and access points in peak condition.
